# What we spend on AI video — and how to spend less

*Based on our real bills. Team of 5, one year (Aug 2026 – Aug 2027).*

We mainly use one tool, **Higgsfield**, to make our videos. There are two cheaper ways to make the **exact same videos**.

| | How we'd use it | Cost / month | Cost / year |
|---|---|---:|---:|
| **1. Higgsfield** *(today)* | Buy credits, generate in their app | **~$1,320** | **~$15,800** |
| **2. fal.ai** | Our own simple dashboard, one monthly bill | **~$860** | **~$10,300** |
| **3. Direct** | Connect to the video makers directly | **~$465** | **~$5,600** |

### The savings

- Switching to **fal.ai** saves about **$5,500 a year**.
- Going **direct** saves about **$10,000 a year**.

### Why
The same 10-second video clip costs **$5.40 on Higgsfield**, **$3.03 on fal**, and **$1.40 direct**. Higgsfield charges roughly **4× the real price**, and credits hide what we're actually paying.

### What we suggest
Build one simple dashboard on **fal.ai** first — one bill, our own spending limits, full visibility. Later, connect our biggest video model directly for the rest of the savings.

*These numbers assume we build the dashboard and the team uses it.*
